## ğŸ“‹ Placeholders to Replace

### Resume Template (`resume.html`)

- `[YOUR FULL NAME]` - Your name in uppercase
- `[Your Title/Role]` - Current position or role
- `[Key Skill 1] & [Key Skill 2]` - Main skills
- `[City, State/Province]` - Your location
- `[Phone Number]` - Your phone number
- `[Email Address]` - Your email
- `[Your Website]` - Personal website (optional)
- `[LinkedIn Profile]` - LinkedIn URL
- `[Job Title]` - Your job titles
- `[Company Name]` - Company names
- `[MM/YYYY]` - Dates in month/year format
- `[Degree Name]` - Your degree(s)
- `[School/University Name]` - Your school(s)
- `[Project Name]` - Your project names
- All bullet points and descriptions

### Cover Letter Template (`coverletter_template.html`)

- `[Your Full Name]` - Your name

- `[Hiring Manager Name]` - Recipient's name
- `[Company Name]` - Company name
- `[Company Address]` - Company address
- `[Position Title]` - Job title you're applying for
- `[Platform]` - Where you found the job posting
- All body paragraph content

## ğŸ’¡ Customization Tips

1. **Keep formatting consistent** - Don't change the HTML structure
2. **Use action verbs** - Start bullet points with strong verbs (Built, Developed, Designed, etc.)
3. **Quantify achievements** - Include numbers when possible (e.g., "Increased efficiency by 25%")
4. **Tailor for each job** - Customize content to match job descriptions
5. **Proofread carefully** - Check for typos and formatting issues
6. **Date format** - Use MM/YYYY format (e.g., 03/2024)
